Biography

David Lawrence Schwimmer was born on November 2, 1966, in Flushing, Queens, New York. He rose to international fame playing Ross Geller on the iconic sitcom Friends (1994-2004), earning him an Emmy nomination and worldwide recognition.

Beyond Friends, David has demonstrated remarkable range as both an actor and director, with notable roles in projects like Band of Brothers, The People v. O.J. Simpson: American Crime Story, and as the voice of Melman in the Madagascar franchise.
